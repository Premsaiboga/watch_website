About Us

Since 1990

At Time Luxe, we believe a watch is more than just a timekeeper — it's a reflection of craftsmanship, heritage, and personal identity. Founded with a passion for horology, our brand curates a handpicked selection of the world’s most exquisite timepieces, designed to transcend generations.

Every watch we showcase tells a story — of precision engineering, timeless design, and a relentless pursuit of perfection. From Swiss-made icons to rare artisanal creations, Time Luxe exists to serve those who value sophistication and legacy.

We don’t sell watches online. We offer experiences — appointments, private viewings, and a chance to connect with your future heirloom in person.

Join us in celebrating the art of time.

Collections

Royal Oak Offshore

The Audemars Piguet Royal Oak is an iconic luxury timepiece that revolutionized watch design with its bold octagonal bezel, exposed screws, and integrated bracelet.

Jacob & Co. Astronomia

The Jacob & Co. Astronomia is a breathtaking masterpiece that fuses haute horology with celestial art, featuring a multi-axis tourbillon and rotating cosmic elements under a sapphire dome.

Riched Mille Mclaren

The Richard Mille McLaren watch is a high-performance, lightweight masterpiece that blends cutting-edge Formula 1 engineering with avant-garde luxury watchmaking.

Patek Philippe Geneve

Patek Philippe Genève is where heritage meets horological artistry—crafting heirlooms that transcend time, not just measure it.

Hublot

Hublot is where bold design meets innovative materials, fusing tradition and future in every striking timepiece.

Rolex Submariner

The Rolex Submariner is the ultimate symbol of precision and prestige—built for deep-sea exploration, yet worn as a timeless icon.
